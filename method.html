Method

In order to accomplish our project, we plan to do our research about the Japanese Occupation through various mediums like the Internet, libraries, the Former Ford Factory and much more. Besides that, we also will try to encompass the traits of a fun board game based on your reviews so that it pleases the crowd and edit the project. Of course, we have organised our timeline to make sure everything is done in time and with quality.
